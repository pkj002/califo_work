# Data Used in This Study

Dataset DOI: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20058559

This repository provides the processed datasets used in the study:

“From intermittent to persistent chill insufficiency in California’s specialty crops”

The archived datasets include:

- GRIDMET-derived chill accumulation time series
- CMIP6 historical and SSP585 climate projection datasets
- ECMWF seasonal forecast-derived chill products
- Crop distribution and spatial aggregation datasets
- Figure-ready processed datasets used in manuscript analyses and visualizations

Meteorological observations were obtained from GRIDMET, while downscaled
CMIP6 climate projections were accessed through the LOCA2 archive.
Seasonal forecast datasets were obtained from the ECMWF Copernicus Climate
Data Store, and crop distribution datasets were derived from the USDA
Cropland Data Layer.

All datasets were spatially aligned on a common WGS84 latitude–longitude grid
and processed for climate-risk analyses and figure generation.
